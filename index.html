About the COMPANY?


Brave Pharma is dedicated to making a healthy difference to the quality of life of our customers and the communities in which they live and work. We empower schools to thrive, prioritise well-being, and leave a lasting impact on the health and success of their communities.

We do that by delivering evidence-based health and wellbeing solutions that support people, communities and workplaces to be healthier. We understand that healthy people are happier and more productive, and our vision is to collaborate with like minded experts to inspire healthy choices.

Health & Lifestyle Screenings

What are Health Screenings?

  • Health screenings are a great way to better understand your employees’ health and wellbeing. We offer a range of screening tests, delivered onsite, to assess cardiovascular risks of employees. This allows us to support companies and their people to take a proactive approach towards health and wellbeing, with the aim of reducing health risks.
  • Access and affordability for our clients is paramount to us as a company; we therefore have created a programme which provides the full range of tests delivered by ourselves at one affordable price; . All sessions are delivered onsite at your workplace to minimise disruption to your employees' working day. You simply promote the programme to your people and fill in our booking form. We’ll do the rest.
  • Empower your employees to make sustainable and informed lifestyle changes that positively impacts their health
